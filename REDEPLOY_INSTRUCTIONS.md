### Option 1: Netlify Drop (Fastest - No Account Needed)

1. **Download your site files:**
   - Go to: https://github.com/etchmuzik/uaelandingpage
   - Click the green "Code" button
   - Click "Download ZIP"
   - Unzip the file on your computer

2. **Deploy to Netlify:**
   - Go to: https://app.netlify.com/drop
   - Drag the unzipped folder onto the page
   - Your site will deploy instantly

### Option 2: Connect GitHub (Recommended - Auto-Deploy)

1. **Log into Netlify:**
   - Go to: https://app.netlify.com/
   - Sign in (or create free account)

2. **Add New Site:**
   - Click "Add new site" → "Import an existing project"
   - Choose "Deploy with GitHub"
   - Authorize Netlify to access your GitHub account

3. **Select Repository:**
   - Search for: `uaelandingpage`
   - Click on: `etchmuzik/uaelandingpage`

4. **Configure Build Settings:**
   - **Branch to deploy:** `main`
   - **Build command:** (leave blank)
   - **Publish directory:** `.` (just a dot)
   - Click "Deploy site"

5. **Wait 1-2 minutes** for deployment to complete

6. **Get Your URL:**
   - Netlify will give you a URL like: `https://random-name-123456.netlify.app`
   - You can customize this in Site settings → Domain management
